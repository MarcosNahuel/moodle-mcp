# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) as its code of conduct.

## Summary

- Be respectful. Disagreements are fine; personal attacks are not.
- Assume good faith. Prefer "I think you misunderstood my point" over "you're wrong".
- No harassment, discrimination, or unwelcome sexual attention — in issues, PRs, commits, or any other project space.
- Keep discussions technical. Politics and ideology have their venues; this is not one of them.

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to the maintainer listed in `package.json`. Reports are handled in confidence. Consequences range from a private warning to a permanent ban from project spaces, depending on severity.
